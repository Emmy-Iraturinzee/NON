Digital Trade Services

Comprehensive services that enable secure, efficient and scalable cross-border digital trade.

Cross-Border Data Flows

Essential for digital trade: data exchange supports supply chains, enables remote work, and fosters innovation across borders.

Clearing & Forwarding

Facilitates movement of goods across international borders by handling customs clearance, logistics, and transportation for freight forwarders or customs agencies.

E-commerce

Buying and selling goods and services over the internet, enabling businesses and consumers to engage globally without physical borders.

E-customs

Paperless business transactions where the Customs Administration provides electronic services to users, streamlining cross-border trade compliance.

Digital Services

Services delivered digitally—software, cloud computing, fintech, education and consulting—expanding market reach for service-based businesses.

Digital Payments

Fintech solutions like online payments, cryptocurrency, and digital wallets that facilitate cross-border transactions and reduce reliance on traditional banking.
